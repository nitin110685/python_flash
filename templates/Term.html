Terms And Conditions

Welcome to our Driving School! Driving School provides our services to you subject to the following conditions. Please read them carefully.

  • Once taken admission will not be cancelled for any reason. Fees will not be refunded.

  • Learning License should be revoked within one month from the day of admission, otherwise you will have to pay the fees.

  • You have to pay full fees at a time.

  • Sunday will be a holiday.

  • If you takes leave, the class will not be resumed.If you want to take a vacation and you say to take it the next day this will not happen We will not take holiday, if it does, then the next day the holiday will be completed by taking more kilometers.

  • Class time can sometimes be 15/20 minutes late (due to traffic / new learners).

  • Ghat training will be done only once. It should be noted that 4 people will be with you.

  • Pick-up drop does not mean coming to the house, you have to come to the nearest intersection from the house.

  • After getting approval for Permanent License, you should come to the office to apply for your permanent licence.

  • If you want extra training, you have to pay the training fees.
